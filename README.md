# Software Advice

Software Advice (https://www.softwareadvice.com/) is a B2B software recommendation platform owned by Gartner. Founded in 2005 and acquired by Gartner in 2014, Software Advice helps businesses find the right software through a combination of expert advisors, user reviews, and curated product comparisons. The platform covers over 300 software categories and offers free one-on-one consultations with industry specialists who guide buyers through the selection process, typically in 15 minutes or less.

## Overview

Software Advice is part of the Gartner Digital Markets network, which also includes Capterra and GetApp. The platform aggregates over 2 million verified user reviews and provides side-by-side comparisons of software products across categories including CRM, ERP, HR, project management, accounting, and more. For software vendors, Software Advice offers lead generation through sponsored product profiles, connecting them with buyers who are actively evaluating solutions. Vendors can list their products, gather reviews, and receive sales-qualified leads from matched buyers.

## Key Features

- Free one-on-one consultations with software advisors across 300+ categories
- Over 2 million verified user reviews with human moderation
- Side-by-side software comparisons with pricing, features, and user ratings
- Vendor listing and sponsored profile program for lead generation
- Buyer-vendor matching based on business needs, company size, and budget
- Highly Qualified Leads (HQL) program connecting vendors with active buyers
- Part of Gartner Digital Markets alongside Capterra and GetApp
